* Does NVEIL AI require coding or data science skills?
NVEIL AI is designed for everyone, without requiring coding or data science skills. Its intuitive interface allows anyone to leverage the power of AI without advanced technical knowledge. Each step is clearly explained to detail the processing performed.

* What is the difference between NVEIL AI and a classic data-viz tool?
NVEIL AI stands out from classic data-viz tools by its ability to automate the visualization creation process from raw data. Unlike traditional tools that require significant manual intervention, NVEIL AI uses custom-developed artificial intelligence to generate interactive visualizations in minutes, making the process faster and accessible to all.

* Does the AI explain its choices? How does explainability work?
Yes, NVEIL AI provides explanations for its choices and recommendations. Explainability is integrated into the process, allowing users to understand how and why certain decisions were made by the AI. This includes visualizations of input data, models used, and generated results, so users can follow the AI’s reasoning and validate its conclusions. Unlike other types of artificial intelligence commonly used today, NVEIL AI does not rely on LLMs for reasoning and processing. Instead, a set of explicit rules and logic is used to guide the AI’s decision-making process.

Average processing times for a visualization depend on the complexity of the data and the processing applied. In general, NVEIL AI aims to provide results in a few seconds to minutes, but this may vary depending on the size and nature of the data.

* What is Explainable AI and how is it implemented in NVEIL?
Explainable artificial intelligence is a set of processes and methods that enable human users to understand and trust the results generated by AI algorithms. It allows the accuracy, fairness, transparency, and results of the model to be characterized in AI-powered decision-making. To meet these requirements, NVEIL AI internally documents the entire creation process for each visualization, allowing control over every step and decision made by the AI.

* How can I upload a file?
To upload a file, simply drag and drop it into the chat, or click on the paperclip icon to the left of the chat bar to open a file manager. You can also use real-time data extracted online by providing us the URL.

* How can I manage my files/data sources? 
There are two ways to manage your files:
- Click on the paperclip icon next to the message input bar to open a window. Here you can add new files, source URLs, and manage active data sources (by checking or unchecking them). 
- By going to the “My Files” tab in the navigation bar (to the left of the chat), you can manage your files (rename them, delete them, re-upload them).

* Are the data transformed in order to produce visualizations?
For certain types of visualizations or queries, data needs to be transformed (filtering, aggregations, arithmetic calculations, etc.). To perform these operations, we rely on the Kedro library (https://kedro.org/), which has enabled us to implement a vast library of transformations.

* What technical stack is used to display visualizations?
For your renderings, we use well-known libraries such as Plotly, VTK, deck.gl, and many others, in order to cover a wide range of possibilities. 

* How do I create a dashboard?
To create a dashboard, you must first generate the visualization using the chat, then export it to a dashboard using the button that appears at the bottom right of the message describing your result. You can then create a dashboard by naming it (or use an existing one). You can then add the visualizations resulting from the chat to this table. To view it, simply select the “Dashboard” tab in the navigation bar to the left of the chat and select the one you want to view.
